# 1

Jesus, stand among us

In Thy risen power;

Let this time of worship

Be a hallowed hour.



# 2

Breathe the Holy Spirit

Into every heart;

Bid the fears and sorrows

From each soul depart.



# 3

Thus with quickened footsteps

We pursue our way,

Watching for the dawning

Of eternal day.
